Hip-Hop's Obsession with Booties

Profanity in hip-hop music is such a common occurrence that it ceases to surprise. However, it is interesting to understand at what point women’s body parts became exclusively objectified in hip-hop music.

This project is a tiny attempt at seeing the popularity of “booties” (and no, it’s not the male booty) in hip-hop music across time (from 2000 to 2020). Using Genius.com’s website and with further help from its API, I created a database of all songs whose lyrics have ‘booty’ in them, along with their data or release and frequency of occurrence.
